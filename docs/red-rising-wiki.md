## 2. The Color hierarchy

Society humanity is genetically engineered into **fourteen Colors** (the Eidmi bioweapon canonically targets "any one of the fourteen"):

- **Gold** — rulers; enhanced strength/intellect; the only Color permitted razors (death penalty otherwise). "Peerless Scarred" = Golds who passed the Institute.
- **Silver** — financiers (e.g., Quicksilver, richest man alive).
- **White** — clergy/judges. **Copper** — bureaucrats. **Blue** — pilots/navigators (crew ships; Orion xe Aquarii). **Yellow** — doctors/scientists. **Green** — programmers. **Violet** — artists/Carvers (body-sculptors; built the Pandemonium Chair). **Orange** — mechanics/Master Makers (Glirastes; razor "Bladeweavers"). **Gray** — soldiers/police (Legio XIII Dragoons, Praetorians, Rhone ti Flavinius). **Brown** — servants. **Obsidian** — engineered warrior caste (Ragnar, Sefi, Valdir, the Valkyrie; post-Rising: the Alltribe/Volk). **Pink** — pleasure/companionship (Sevro's mother; Aurae [recall: a Pink]). **Red** — miners/manual labor (Darrow: a Helldiver of Lykos, Mars). HighReds surface labor, lowReds the mines.

## 3. Polities and factions (as of ~755 PCE)

- **The Society (pre-743)** — the Color-caste interplanetary empire ruled by Sovereign **Octavia au Lune** from Luna. Overthrown in the Fall of Luna (Nov 743).
- **The Solar Republic** — the Rising's successor state: Luna, Earth, Mars (+ Phobos/Deimos until late 754), Mercury 753–754. Sovereign **Virginia au Augustus**; Senate on Luna; ArchImperator **Darrow**. Military: Free Legions + White Fleet (expeditionary), Home Guard (Luna/Earth), Ecliptic Guard (Mars/Belt).
- **The Society Remnant** — Gold loyalists who fled to Mercury/Venus after the Fall. Led by the **Ash Lord (Magnus au Grimmus)**, then his daughter **Dictator Atalantia au Grimmus** (from 750); **Lysander au Lune** rises to claim the Sovereignty within it (marries into alliance with Atalantia after retaking Mercury). Core strength: Ash Armada, Ash Legions, Venus/Mercury industry, house fleets (Grimmus, Carthii, Votum, Valii-Rath, Bellona remnants, Falthe, Saud).
- **The Rim Dominion** — the outer-planets Golds (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune), seat at Ilium (Jovian moons). Independent after the Battle of Ilium (743). Led by **Helios au Lux** and House **Raa** (Romulus †743 [recall: ritual suicide after the Dockyards lie was exposed]; Dido; Diomedes; Seraphina †754). Military: Dust, Dragon, Shadow Armadas; phalanxes. Joined the Remnant against the Republic in 753 (motivated by Darrow's Ganymede Dockyards deception), then flipped to the **Covenant** after the Kalyke Massacre (755).
- **The Alltribe / Volk** — Obsidians under Queen **Sefi** who withdrew from Republic service after the Mercury Iron Rain (753) and seized territory on Mars **[recall: Sefi is murdered by Volsung Fá in *Dark Age*]**; later ruled by **Volga Fjorgan**, crowned Volk queen on Europa (Feb 755) after Darrow defeats **Volsung Fá** (真 Vagnar Hefga, Ragnar's father, an Ascomanni king and Atlas's cat's-paw).
- **The Ascomanni** — "dark-side" Obsidian raiders of the deep ice, weaponized by Atlas au Raa; fielded hundreds of warships at the Kalyke Massacre.
- **Vox Populi** — radical lowColor populist movement on Luna (demagogue **Publius cu Caraval**); with the **Syndicate** crime network (queen: **Lilath au Faran** [recall — verify]; the "Duke of Hands" fenced the kidnapped children [recall]) executed the **Day of Red Doves** coup (Jan 754).
- **The Daughters of Athena** — anti-Gold resistance operating in the Rim [recall: led by Athena; Aurae is one]; Covenant signatory.
- **The Covenant (755)** — Republic + Rim + Volk + Daughters of Athena united against the Society Remnant after Lysander's atrocities (Kalyke aftermath, Sack of Demeter).

## 4. Key figures (roles & fates through *Light Bringer*)

### Republic
- **Darrow of Lykos / au Andromedus** — Red Helldiver carved into a Gold; the Reaper. ArchImperator 743–753 (stripped after the unsanctioned Mercury Iron Rain; arrest warrant), restored later. Heart attack at the Ladon; escapes Mercury on the *Archimedes* (Feb 754); kills Volsung Fá on Europa (755). Married to Virginia; son **Pax**.
- **Virginia au Augustus (Mustang)** — Sovereign of the Republic. Captured in the Day of Red Doves coup (Jan 754), rescued mid-Feb by Niobe au Telemanus's Ecliptic Guard; personally commands the Siege of Phobos defense and negotiates its surrender.
- **Sevro au Barca** — Darrow's blood-brother, the Goblin; Howler prime; Imperator, White Fleet. At Gorgon Isle (Nov 753); returns to Luna hunting his kidnapped children **[recall: captured; imprisoned much of *Light Bringer*; his daughter is among the kidnapped]**.
- **Victra au Barca (née Julii)** — Sevro's wife; Julii industrial empire; Imperator, Ecliptic Guard; counter-Iron-Rain at Phobos while pregnant.
- **Orion xe Aquarii** — Blue; Navarch of the Republic navy; Imperator 1st Fleet 743–754. Tortured three weeks by Gorgons; takes the Storm Gods above the primary horizon at the Ladon; killed by Darrow via Glirastes' failsafe (Jan 5, 754).
- **Cadus Harnassus** — Orange; ArchLegate of the Free Legions (746–754), Darrow's deputy; survives Mercury aboard the *Archimedes*.
- **Alexandar au Arcos** — Lorn's grandson, first of the Knights of Elysium; hero of the Tyche gravLoop stand; killed by Lysander in single combat during the Long Night (Feb 754).
- **Thraxa au Telemanus** — veteran of Earth (saved Valdir over the Bay of Bengal); escapes Mercury with Darrow. Family: **Kavax** (spine broken by Apollonius at Phobos), **Niobe**, **Daxo †** [recall: murdered by the Vox on the Day of Red Doves].
- **Colloway xe Char** — legendary Blue ace; leads 2,011 survivors' escape from Mercury; captains the *Dejah Thoris* at Phobos.
- **Dancer O'Faran** — Red senator, ex-Sons of Ares; Rat War commander (liberated Lagalos 751); leads the Senate opposition to Darrow **[recall: poisoned on the Day of Red Doves]**.
- Others: **Sefi** (Obsidian queen — see Alltribe), **Valdir** (Obsidian war hero, freed at Phobos), **Holiday & Trigg ti Nakamura** (Grays; Trigg †742), **Quicksilver/Regulus ag Sun** (Silver plutocrat; Sun Industries; built Godkiller armor; *Tabula Rasa* escape project), **Glirastes** (Orange Master Maker of Heliopolis — defects to Darrow 753, built the Storm God failsafe, turned back by Lysander before the Long Night), **Screwface** (Howler, escapes Mercury), **Rhonna** (Darrow's Red niece, lancer).

### Society Remnant
- **Magnus au Grimmus, the Ash Lord** — burned Rhea (701); commanded Scepter then Ash Armada; poisoned from ~750 by a scheme Apollonius ran from Deepgrave; died Nov 15, 753 at Gorgon Isle (suicide pill as Darrow/Sevro/Apollonius closed in).
- **Atalantia au Grimmus** — Dictator; concealed her father's condition three years; commanded the Battle of Caliban ambush and the Ash Rain/Ladon; loses Ajax (Phobos) and is outmaneuvered by Lysander's rise.
- **Lysander au Lune** — Octavia's grandson, memory-wiped by the Pandemonium Chair as a child; hidden decade with Cassius on the *Archimedes*; destroys the desert Storm God at the Ladon; triggers the Long Night; retakes Mercury; kills Alexandar, later **Atlas and Cassius**; burns Demeter's Garter; claims the Sovereignty.
- **Atlas au Raa** — the Fear Knight; commands Legio Zero Pavor Nocturnus (Gorgons); Rat War, Mercury guerrilla campaign, engineered the Ascomanni/Volsung Fá plot; killed by Lysander (755).
- **Ajax au Grimmus** — Atalantia's protégé [recall: her son? verify — treat as protégé/heir], Legate of Legio X Pardus; dies at the Siege of Phobos (Dec 754).
- **Apollonius au Valii-Rath, the Minotaur** — betrayed by the Ash Lord (747 suicide mission), Deepgrave prisoner, freed by Darrow's faction; avenged himself via poison; seizes the Venus Dockyards; breaks Kavax at Phobos. Brother **Tharsus**.
- **The Votum family** — Mercury's ruling house: **Scorpio** (bisected by Darrow at the Ladon), **Cicero** (Scorpion Legion legate, flips politically post-Mercury), **Ovidius & Porcia** (drowned at Tyche).
- Others: **Asmodeus au Carthii** (killed in the Venus Dockyards battle), **Kalindora au San** [recall: dies of Ladon wounds, confesses about Lysander's parents], **Rhone ti Flavinius** (Praetorian Gray legend), **Julia au Bellona**, **Valeria au Carthii** (Lysander's installed Primus).

### Rim
- **Helios au Lux** — senior Imperator (Dust Armada, *Dustmaker*, Phoenix Phalanx) [recall: dies in *Light Bringer*, at Demeter's Garter — verify].
- **Dido au Raa** — Dragon Armada Imperator [recall: killed at/after Phobos — verify].

- **Seraphina au Raa** — Eleventh Dustwalkers; killed at the Ladon (railgun round) during Lysander's Storm God assault.

## 5. Worlds gazetteer (battle-relevant)

- **Luna** — Society capital; the Fall (743); Block Wars (744–746); Day of Red Doves (754). Citadel of Light, the Crescent Vault (where the Storm God records were kept).
- **Earth** — Grimmus ancestral seat New Sparta (Africa); Khardung La; Gibraltar; liberated 746–747 (Wolf Rain); falls back to the Remnant March 754 (Twins of South Pacifica railgun installation).
- **Mars** — Darrow's homeworld (Lykos mine). Cities: **Agea** (capital; Lion's Rain 742; liberated ~748), **Olympia** (Bellona seat; siege 747; given to Obsidians), **New Thebes** (nuked from orbit by the Ash Lord), **Zephyria** (battle 747). Rat War in the mines (748/9–751). Valkyrie Spires (south pole, Lovelock-made). Moons **Phobos** (battles 743, 754) and **Deimos** (battle 742).
- **Mercury** — Society Remnant fortress world; two continents referenced: **Borealis** (Darrow's Iron Rain, July 753) and **Helios** (the Ladon campaign). Helios geography: **Tyche** (NW coastal capital on the **Sycorax Sea**; Talarian Peninsula; Water Colossus; drowned Jan 754), **Heliopolis** (pop. 20M; Storm Wall; between **Hesperides** (N) and **Aigle** (S) mountains on the Bay of Sirens/**Caliban Sea**; Hippodrome, Via Triumphia, Mound of Votum), **Waste of Ladon** (central desert), Plains of Caduceus, Red Reach (nuked), a Tyche↔Heliopolis subterranean **gravLoop**. Seven Storm Gods were buried under the polar ice by House Lune as anti-Votum insurance.
- **Venus** — Remnant industrial heart: **Venus Dockyards** (Carthii), **Gorgon Isle** (Grimmus citadel; battle Nov 753).
- **Ceres** — dwarf-planet trade hub; Hanging Market; one of three Society helium-3 War Reserves; battle ~750; retaken by Diomedes 754.
- **Jupiter / Ilium** — the Rim's seat: Ganymede (Dockyards, secretly destroyed by Darrow 743), Io, Europa (Volk coronation 755), **Kalyke** (massacre Jan 755), **Demeter's Garter** (agri-belt, sacked by Lysander 755).
- **Saturn** — **Rhea** (nuked 701, the Burning; pop. was ~60M); Titan [recall: Second Moon Lords' front].
- **Triton & Pluto** — believed to still have active Storm Gods (only worlds, as of the 8th century PCE).
